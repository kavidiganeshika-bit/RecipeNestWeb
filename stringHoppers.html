String Hoppers

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Hot water
  • Salt

Instructions

To prepare string hoppers, start by mixing rice flour with a pinch of salt. Gradually add hot water and mix until it forms a soft, non-sticky dough. While the dough is still warm, place it into a string hopper press. Press the dough in circular shapes onto small mats or plates. Steam the string hoppers for about 5–7 minutes until they are cooked and soft. Serve hot with coconut sambol, dhal curry, or any curry of your choice.
