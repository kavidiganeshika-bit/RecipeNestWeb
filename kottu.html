Kottu

Ingredients

  • Roti pieces
  • Vegetables
  • Egg or meat

Instructions

Start by cutting godamba roti into small pieces. Heat a large pan or griddle and add oil. Fry onions, green chilies, and vegetables. Add eggs or meat if desired and cook thoroughly. Then add the chopped roti pieces and mix everything together. Use two metal spatulas to chop and mix continuously. Add spices and sauces for flavor. Cook until everything is well combined and hot. Serve immediately.
