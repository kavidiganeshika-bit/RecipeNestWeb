Milk Tea

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Instructions

Boil water in a pot and add tea leaves. Let it brew until the tea becomes strong and dark. Add sugar according to your taste. Then pour in milk and bring the mixture to a boil again. Stir well and allow it to simmer for a few minutes. Strain the tea into cups and serve hot. You can adjust the strength by adding more tea or milk
