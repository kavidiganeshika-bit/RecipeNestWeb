Roti

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions

In a bowl, mix flour with salt and gradually add water to form a soft dough. Knead the dough well until smooth. Divide it into small balls and flatten each ball into a round shape using your hands or a rolling pin. Heat a pan and cook each roti on both sides until golden brown spots appear. You can add coconut or chopped onions for extra flavor. Serve with sambol or curry.
